About Me

Hello! I'm Xadiel, a passionate software developer from San Juan, Puerto Rico. I hold a degree in Computer Science from the Polytechnic University of Puerto Rico, where I graduated in 2023. My professional journey has led me to work with a diverse set of technologies across various industries. I've gained substantial experience working with .NET, Microsoft SQL Server, Oracle DB, and in another role, I delved into Spring Boot with Java, MariaDB, and Power BI. Currently, I'm leveraging Salesforce, Vlocity, Copado, and Heroku in my work. I'm always on the lookout for exciting business ideas and innovative projects.
